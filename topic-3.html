Topic 3

專有名詞與發音糾正

Terminology & Pronunciation Drill

3.1 精準發音特訓

在流暢朗讀之後，您可能會發現講稿中有幾個「舌頭容易打結」的單字，或是發音不確定是否標準的專業術語。這時候我們可以進入特訓模式，請 AI 擔任發音教練，帶領您進行單字反覆練習。

Prompt 3: Pronunciation Drill
I want to do a pronunciation drill for a few challenging words and technical terms from my script. Here are the words: [在此輸入您想練習的單字，例如: 1. Entrepreneurship 2. Quantitative 3. Unprecedented] Please act as a supportive pronunciation coach. For each word, read it slowly and clearly once, then ask me to repeat it back to you. If I mispronounce it, kindly point out the specific syllable or sound I should adjust, and let's try again. Let's do this one word at a time.

使用提示：填寫您覺得難唸的 3 到 5 個單字即可。這是一個互動環節，AI 唸完後會等您開口，請專注於模仿它的重音（Stress）與語調（Intonation）。
