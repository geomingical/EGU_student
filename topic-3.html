Topic 3

專有名詞與發音糾正

Terminology & Pronunciation Drill

3.1 精準發音特訓

在流暢朗讀之後，您可能會發現講稿中有幾個「舌頭容易打結」的單字，或是發音不確定是否標準的專業術語。這時候我們可以進入特訓模式，請 AI 擔任發音教練，帶領您進行單字反覆練習。

Prompt 3: Pronunciation Drill
I want to do a pronunciation drill for a few challenging words and technical terms from my script. Here are the words: For example: 1. Seismometer, 2. Accelerate, 3. Power Spectral Please act as a supportive pronunciation coach. For each word: - Say the word slowly and clearly once - Break it into syllables and highlight the stress - Then ask me to repeat it After I respond: - Give only 1 or 2 focused corrections (if needed) - Point out the exact sound or syllable to improve - Then let me try again Let's go one word at a time. You should use simple English. Wait for my response before moving on.

使用提示：每一次填寫您覺得難唸的 3 到 5 個單字即可(或請GPT直接幫你從docx裡面去找需要練習的單字)，分批練習。這是一個互動環節，AI 唸完後會等您開口，請專注於模仿它的重音（Stress）與語調（Intonation）。建議每天練習
