Topic 4

角色扮演問答練習

Role-Play Q&A Simulation

4.1 實戰模擬演練

這是最具挑戰性但也最有價值的練習。我們將請 AI 扮演面試官、教授或專業人士，針對您剛剛準備的主題，對您進行 5 個問題的問答測試。這能大幅提升您臨場反應與未經腳本準備的口語對答能力。

Prompt 4: Role-play & Final Evaluation
Let's do a role-play Q&A session based on the topic we've been working on. Act as either a student or a domain expert in this field. Please ask me exactly 5 questions related to my topic, one at a time. Wait for my answer before asking the next question. Do not correct my grammar during the Q&A so we can maintain the flow. After all 5 questions are finished, provide a comprehensive evaluation of my performance. Please evaluate: 1. Overall clarity and confidence. 2. Areas where I can improve my vocabulary or grammar. 3. One key takeaway to focus on for my next practice session.

使用提示：在回答問題時，嘗試運用 Topic 1 學到的新單字。如果聽不懂 AI 的問題，您可以隨時用英文說「Could you please rephrase that?」或「Could you speak a bit slower?」，這也是非常重要的溝通技巧。
